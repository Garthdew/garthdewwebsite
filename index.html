Garth Dew | Musician & Visual Artist

Artistic Statement

My creation is done for its own sake. I notice ideas as they arise and develop them. My projects explore generative systems, minimalism, randomness, storytelling, and play—often merging digital tools with organic material across music and visual art. I find ideas and concepts more interesting than form. But finishing and publishing projects is an essential part of creative journey.

Creative Manifesto>

  • Possibility > Limitation
  • Ideas > Form
  • Randomness > Certainty
  • Restraints > Unrestricted
  • Systems > Chaos
  • Minimalism > Excess
  • Play > Work
  • Stories > Facts
  • Finishing > Tinkering

Biography

After a brief career as a professional athlete, I reinvented my life through creativity. I earned a degree in music production before becoming a filmmaker, and now run a commercial film and content company while creating original music and visual art. My early original projects focussed on documentary-style films and street photography. Recently, I’ve returned to my musical roots, experimenting with generative systems, creative restraints, play, improvisation, coding, minimalism and randomness. These experiments have grown into new forms, including visual art.
