Hello, I'm Garth

Creation is done for its own sake. Ideas are noticed as they come through and acted upon. Interests include randomness, generative systems, minimalism, stories, and play—spanning multiple audio/visual media, merging virtual and material elements. Ideas are more compelling than form, but finishing and sharing projects is essential.

Creative Guidelines:

  • Possibility > Limitation
  • Ideas > Form
  • Randomness > Certainty
  • Restraints > Unrestricted
  • Systems > Chaos
  • Minimalism > Excess
  • Play > Work
  • Stories > Facts
  • Publishing > Withholding
