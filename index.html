About

I am an artist and filmmaker from Northern England.

After a brief career as a professional athlete, I focussed on creativity. I earned a degree in music production before becoming a filmmaker, and now run a commercial film company while creating original sound and visual art.

My sound and visual art explores generative systems, minimalism, randomness, restraint, improvisation, and play—often merging organic material with digital processes.

My orginal and commercial films are mostly centered around storytelling in a documentary style. I have created commercial films for brands such as Neve, Volvo, Jaguar and Speedo. My original documentaries have been featured on the BBC and at Kendal Mountain.
