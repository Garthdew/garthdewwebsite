Garth Dew is a UK-based artist

Statement

My creation is done for its own sake. I notice ideas as they arise and act upon them. My projects explore generative systems, minimalism, randomness, storytelling, and play—often merging digital tools with organic material across music and visual art. Ideas are more interesting than form, but finishing and publishing projects is essential.

Manifesto

  1. Possibility > Limitation
  2. Ideas > Form
  3. Randomness > Certainty
  4. Restraints > Unrestricted
  5. Systems > Chaos
  6. Minimalism > Excess
  7. Play > Work
  8. Stories > Facts
  9. Finishing > Tinkering

Bio

After a brief career as a professional athlete, I reinvented my life through creativity. I earned a degree in music production before becoming a filmmaker, and now I run a commercial film and content company while creating original art across multiple media. My early work focused on storytelling in a documentary style, including short films about athletes and explorations in street photography. Recently, I’ve returned to my musical roots, experimenting with generative processes, creative restraints, play, improvisation, coding, minimalism and randomness. These experiments have grown into new forms, including generative visual art.
